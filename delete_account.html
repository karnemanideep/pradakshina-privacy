🙏 Overview

At Pradakshina by CRUX Technologies, we respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, this page explains exactly how to do so, what gets removed, and how long it takes.

Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read the information below carefully before proceeding.

🗑️ How to Delete Your Account

You can request the deletion of your Pradakshina account using either of the following methods:

1
From Within the App

Open Pradakshina → Go to your Profile screen → Tap Settings → Select “Delete Account” → Confirm deletion. Your account and all data will be scheduled for removal immediately.

2
By Email Request

Send an email to cruxtechnologies8@gmail.com with the subject line: “Delete My Account — Pradakshina”. Include the email address linked to your account so we can locate and remove your data.

📋 How to Request Data Deletion

If you only want specific data removed (without deleting your entire account), you can also submit a data deletion request:

  • Email us at cruxtechnologies8@gmail.com with the subject: “Data Deletion Request — Pradakshina”
  • Specify which data you want removed — e.g., all session records, specific photos, or profile information
  • We will respond within 3 business days to confirm receipt of your request
  • The requested data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

You can also delete individual sessions and photos directly within the app at any time without contacting us.

☁️ What Data Gets Deleted

When your account deletion is processed, the following data is permanently and irreversibly removed from our systems:

  • Firebase Authentication — Your sign-in credentials and Google account link are removed. You will no longer be able to log in with this account.
  • Cloud Firestore — Session Records — All your Pradakshina session data is deleted, including temple names, locations, Sankalpas (prayer wishes), round counts, and timestamps.
  • Firebase Storage — Photos — All photos you uploaded or captured during your temple visits are permanently deleted from cloud storage.
  • Profile Information — Your saved name, profile details, and any app preferences stored under your account are removed.
  • Reminder Settings — All notification schedules and reminder configurations linked to your account are deleted.

⚠️ Note: Data that has been anonymized or aggregated (with no link to your identity) may be retained for service improvement. No identifiable personal data is kept after deletion.

⏳ Deletion Timeline

We are committed to processing all deletion requests promptly:

  • Acknowledgement: You will receive a confirmation email within 3 business days of your request
  • Active Data: Your account and all primary data (sessions, photos, profile) will be deleted within 7 days of confirmation
  • Backup Systems: Any remaining copies in backup or disaster-recovery systems will be fully purged within 30 days

After the 30-day period, no personal data associated with your account will remain in any of our systems.

⚠️ Before You Delete

Please keep the following in mind before deleting your account:

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed
  • All your Sacred History records and temple photos will be lost forever
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Pradakshina again in the future
  • Deleting the app from your device does not delete your account or data — you must follow the steps above

If you are experiencing issues with the app, consider reaching out to us first — we may be able to help resolve the problem without losing your data.
