🙏 Introduction

Welcome to Pradakshina, a sacred prayer app developed by CRUX Technologies. We care deeply about your privacy and handle your personal information with full honesty and respect.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe when you use the Pradakshina app. By using our app, you agree to what is written in this policy.

Your prayer journey is personal. We collect only what is needed to give you the best experience in the app.

📋 Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Account Info: When you sign in with Google, we get your name, email, and profile photo from your Google account.
  • Your Name: The name you save inside the app for your profile.
  • Session Records: Details of each Pradakshina you do — temple name, location, your Sankalpa (prayer wish), and round count.
  • Photos: Photos you take or upload during your temple visit. These are saved securely in the cloud.
  • Reminder Settings: The prayer reminders you set inside the app.
  • Basic Device Info: Simple device details needed for the app to work properly.

We do not collect your bank details, your exact GPS location, or any information without you doing something in the app first.

🔧 How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to make the Pradakshina app work well for you:

  • To log you in safely and keep your account secure
  • To save and show your past Pradakshina records (Sacred History)
  • To store and show your temple photos in your gallery
  • To send you prayer reminders based on what you set
  • To show your name and photo on your profile
  • To fix bugs and make the app better over time

We do not use your data to show you ads, sell your data to anyone, or share your prayer records with any third party.

☁️ Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase — a trusted and secure cloud platform by Google:

  • Firebase Authentication — Secure Google Sign In
  • Cloud Firestore — Encrypted database (Mumbai region, asia-south1)
  • Firebase Storage — Secure photo storage

All data is sent over the internet using HTTPS — meaning it is fully encrypted and safe. Only you can access your sessions and photos after logging in. Nobody else can see your personal data.

We use strong security measures to protect your information from any unauthorized access or misuse.

🤝 Third-Party Services

Pradakshina uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In — For authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase — For data storage and backend services. Firebase Privacy Policy

We do not use any ad networks, tracking tools, or social media trackers in the current version of the app.

👤 Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: View all your session data and photos within the app at any time
  • Deletion: Delete individual sessions or photos from within the app
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Sign Out: Sign out at any time from the Profile screen
  • Reminders: Disable or modify all notification settings at any time

To request complete account deletion, please email us at cruxtechnologies8@gmail.com with subject "Delete My Account — Pradakshina".

👶 Children's Privacy

Pradakshina is meant for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children below 13 years of age. If you think a child under 13 has used the app and shared their information, please write to us at cruxtechnologies8@gmail.com and we will delete that data right away.

🔔 Permissions We Request

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos of your temple visits (optional)
  • Photo Library / Storage: To save and access temple photos (optional)
  • Internet: Required for sign in and data sync
  • Notifications: For sacred reminders you set (optional)
  • Exact Alarms: To fire reminders at exact scheduled times (optional)

All permissions are optional except Internet access. You can use the core counting features without granting camera or notification permissions.
